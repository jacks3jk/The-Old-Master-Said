2	1	There are ways but the Way is uncharted; There are names but not nature in words: Nameless indeed is the source of creation. But things have a mother and she has a name. The secret waits for the insight Of eyes unclouded by longing; Those who are bound by desire See only the outward container. These two come paired but distinct By their names.Of all things profound, say that their pairing is deepest,The gate to the root of the world.
